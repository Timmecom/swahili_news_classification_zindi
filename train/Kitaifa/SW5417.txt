 WASICHANA wameonekana kung ara katika matokeo ya upimaji kitaifa kwa watahiniwa wa Kidato cha Pili kwa mwaka 2018 baada ya kushinda nafasi saba kati ya wanafunzi 10 bora.Kwa upande wa watahiniwa wa Darasa la Nne wavulana na wasichana wamekabana koo kwa uwiano sawa. Akitangaza matokeo ya Kidato cha Pili kwa mwaka 2018, Katibu Mtendaji wa Baraza la Mitihani la Tanzania (Necta), Dk Charles Msonde alisema wanafunzi hao wamepangwa kulingana na wastani wa pointi kwa masomo saba waliyofanya vizuri zaidi.Aliwataja wanafunzi walifanya vizuri kitaifa, Dk Msonde alisema nafasi ya kwanza imeshikwa na Layla Atokwete wa Shule ya Sekondari Canossa, Bernadetha Kabuche (St Aloysius Girls), Lissa Assenga (Feza Girls), Janeth Shirima (St Aloysius Girls), Angela Massawe (Marian Girls), Careen Ngowi (Henry Gogarty), Samson Mwakabage (Jude), Justina Gerald ( Canossa), Johnson Sanga (Marian Boys) na Samelnego Silaa (Libermann Boys).Aliwataja wasichana 10 bora kuwa ni Layla (Canossa), Bernadetha (St Aloysius Girls), Lissa (Feza Girls), Janeth (St Aloysius Girls), Angela Marian Girls), Careen (Henry Gogarty), Justina (Canossa), Glory Kitaraga ( Marian Girls), Neema Jovin (Canossa) na Lilian Richard (Precious Blood). Dk Msonde aliwataja wavulana 10 bora kuwa ni Samson (Jude), Johnson (Marian Boys), Samelnego (Libermann Boys) Gift Christian (Marian Boys), Isaya Rukamya (feza Boys), John Rugemalila (Marian Boys), Edgar Magori (Morning Star), Joshua Mafie (Magnificat), Collins Evans (Feza Boys) na Edwin Silayo ( Jude) Dk Msonde alitaja shule 10 zilizofanya vizuri kuwa ni St Francis Girls (Mbeya), Thamas More Machrina na Bright Future Girls (Dar es Salaam), St, Monica Moshoro Girls (Arusha), Bethel Sabs Girls (Iringa), Centennial (Pwani), St Augustine- Tagaste(Dar), Anwarite Girls(Kilimanjaro), St Mary s Ulete (Iringa) na St Aloysius Girls (Pwani).Alizitaja shule zilizofanya vibaya kuwa ni Luagala (Mtwara), Mashinde(Tanga), Magindu ( Pwani), Imbafi (Dodoma), Korona (Arusha), Thaqalain (Dar), Miteja ( Lindi), Kwashemshi na Mdando (Tanga) na Ocean (Mtwara). Darasa la Nne Kwa matokeo ya mtihani wa upimaji darasa la nne, Dk Msonde alitaja wanafunzi waliiongoza kitaifa kuwa ni Bahati Katema wa Shule ya Msingi Glory to God, Mwakombo (Twibhoki), Musa Musa (Glory to God), Dylan Mfuruki (Bohari), Nelia George (Lwasa), Annastazia Kashaga (Rishor), Dorcas Kaaya (Kishapuy), Eligius Kajoki (Mawingu), Mansa Njola (Glory) na Shinje Lutambi (Ng wakitolyo).Aliwataja wasichana 10 bora kuwa ni Mwakombo(Twibhoki), Nelia (Lwasa), Annastazia (Rishor), Dorcas (Kishapuy), Shinje (Ng wakitolyo), Melesiana Izayas (Chambale), Lilian Kipara(Lwasa), Joanitha Izayas (Chambale), Wanzogia Shan (Samunge) na Jackline Kessy (Kadama). Pia aliwataja wavulana 10 bora kuwa ni Bahati (Glory to God), Musa (Glory to God), Dylan (Bohari), Eligius (Mawingu), Mansa (Glory),Kelvin MAzuguni(Capital), Samingo Imesiapa(Mokilal), Mussa Waziri (kwampunda), Nesphory John (Rishor) na Roderick Lorry (charm Modern).Dk Msonde alitaja shule 10 bora na mikoa inakotoka kuwa ni Bohari na St Severine (Kagera), Twibhoki (Mara), Kemebos (Kagera), Wema (Geita), Imani (Kilimanjaro), Peaceland (Mwanza), Rocken Hill (Shinyanga), Musabe (Mwanza) na Yakin (Arusha). Alitaja mikoa 10 iliyofanya vizuri kuwa ni Kagera, Dar es Salaam, Kilimanjaro, Arusha, Njombe, Iringa, Ruvuma, Mtwara, Morogoro, Manyara wakati halmashauri zilizofanya vizuri ni Arusha (Mjini), Muleba, Moshi (Mjini), Ilala (Mjini), Ulanga, Mji Makambako, Korogwe Mji, Missenyi, Ilala (Vijijini) na Bukoba Manispaa.
